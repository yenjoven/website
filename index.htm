About Montalban

Rodriguez, officially the Municipality of Rodriguez (Tagalog: Bayan ng Rodriguez), formerly known and still commonly known as Montalban, is a 1st-class municipality in Rizal, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 443,954 people, making it the most populous municipality in the country.

The town is located on the slopes of the Sierra Madre mountain range and is the largest in Rizal, covering 312.70 square kilometers. It is home to the Montalban Gorge, part of the Pamitinan Protected Landscape.

Geography

The town consists of mountains, hills, and valleys, with Mount Irid being the highest peak at 1,469 meters. It borders San Mateo, Rizal, Antipolo, Quezon City, and Bulacan.
